Historic Issues

There are a number of historic issues facing the Woolen Mill dam

Current Dam The current dam is not considered historic (see Dam history) by any history organization including the National Register of Historic Places, and the Wisconsin Historical Society. This is because it was built in the late 1930s, and would only serve the Woolen Mill for less than 30 years before the mill went bankrupt. In addition it was built during a period of prolific dam construction in the U.S, and its design and construction are very modern and common all over the country.
West limestone masonry wall While the dam is not historic, the western limestone wing wall arguably is. It is original to the mill and in poor shape (see Engineering Issues). The need to increase the dams spillway capacity threatens this wall with demolition.
